How to Play

Movement

  • W A S D — move
  • Shift — slow walk (quiet)
  • Ctrl — crouch (lower profile)
  • Mouse — look

Interact

  • E or Left Click — doors / secure civilian / collect intel
  • F or Right Click — toggle flashlight
  • Tab — floor plan
  • Esc — pause

Tactics

  • Hostiles see in a cone. Stay out of line-of-sight.
  • Crouch + slow walk to drop detection faster.
  • Light gives you visibility but reveals you.
  • Close doors behind you to break sight.

Win & Lose

  • Win: secure all civilians, reach extraction.
  • Bonus: collect intel folders.
  • Lose: detection bar fills to 100%.
